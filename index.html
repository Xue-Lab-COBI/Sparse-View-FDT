Compressive Fourier-Domain Intensity Coupling (C-FOCUS)

Renzhi He, Yucheng Li, Brianna Urbina, Jiandi Wan, Yi Xue*

University of California, Davis

Paper Code

Abstract

Two-photon microscopy is a powerful tool for in vivo imaging, but its imaging depth is typically limited to a few hundred microns due to tissue scattering. Here, we introduce C-FOCUS, an active scattering correction approach that integrates Fourier-domain intensity modulation with compressive sensing for two-photon microscopy, achieving over 20-fold intensity enhancement and enabling imaging beyond 900 μm in vivo.
